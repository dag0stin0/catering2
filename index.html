01 — The Philosophy

Our Story

The best food is made to pair with drinks and share with friends in the spaces between home and the street. We call ours The Pub.

Our kitchen runs on three words: familiarity, simplicity, quality. We respect the traditions — and when we put our own spin on something, it's only to make the original stronger.

For private events, the philosophy holds — passed apps for a happy hour, a casual meal that keeps coming, a seated dinner with courses, or a communal beefsteak in the old New York tradition. Every format is built so your guests eat, drink, talk, and stay.

Let's build something worth showing up for.

— The PubKey Team
